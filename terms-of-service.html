1. Overview

NOVA7 enables real-time aid coordination, peer-to-peer support, secure donations, and verified identity services for people affected by disasters or living in under-resourced areas. The platform is free to access for eligible users.

2. Eligibility

To use NOVA7, you must:

  • Be at least 13 years old (or the age of digital consent in your country)
  • Provide accurate registration information
  • Use the platform only for lawful and humanitarian purposes
  • Not impersonate another individual or submit false documentation

If you’re registering on behalf of an organization, you confirm that you have authority to bind that organization to these Terms.

3. User Accounts

3.1. Registration

You must create an account and may be required to complete identity verification, which may include government ID uploads or biometric checks (where supported).

3.2. Security

You are responsible for safeguarding your account credentials. Notify us immediately at office@worlddisastercenter.org if you suspect unauthorized use.

3.3. Termination

We may suspend or terminate your account if you violate these Terms or engage in harmful, fraudulent, or abusive behavior.

4. Use of Services

By using NOVA7, you agree to:

  • Respect other users and refrain from harassment, misinformation, or exploitation
  • Not upload harmful code, interfere with services, or misuse community tools
  • Only post accurate, verifiable requests for help or support
  • Acknowledge that financial contributions (“love”) are traceable and voluntary

Note: NOVA7 is not a banking service. Any wallet or payment features are designed to facilitate verified humanitarian support only.

5. Community Standards

We are building a respectful, safe, and empowering environment. Users must not:

  • Post discriminatory, violent, or threatening content
  • Solicit funds outside verified channels
  • Abuse platform tools for scams or fake campaigns
  • Disrupt local aid coordination efforts

WDC reserves the right to moderate, remove, or flag any content that violates these principles.

6. Data and Privacy

All user data is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

We do not sell personal data. By using NOVA7, you consent to data processing required for:

  • Verifying identity
  • Matching help requests to available aid
  • Supporting real-time mapping, alerts, and analytics
  • Ensuring accountability in all transfers and donations
