About Us

The World Disaster Center is a global non-profit organization registered in the United States, Canada, and Austria. NOVA7 is our mission-critical platform designed to deliver real-time support, resources, and community-driven assistance to underprivileged and disaster-affected populations worldwide.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Your privacy and safety are core to our mission. We are committed to protecting your personal data through transparency, responsible practices, and secure technologies.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

1.1. Personal Data You Provide:

  • Name, email, phone number, country, and language preferences
  • Biometric data (if enabled) for secure identity and wallet verification
  • Profile information, including uploaded documents (e.g., KYC, ID)

1.2. Automatically Collected Data:

  • IP address, browser type, location (if permitted)
  • Device data and usage statistics
  • Transaction history and community interactions (posts, comments, support given/received)

1.3. Third-Party Data:

We may collect or verify information from trusted partners (e.g., government registries, humanitarian aid networks).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Deliver support services in real time based on geolocation and needs
  • Verify identities and prevent fraud
  • Power community functions like posting, commenting, and giving support
  • Generate impact reports to improve aid delivery
  • Contact you with important updates or security notifications

We do not sell your data.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We only share your data when necessary and with trusted parties:

  • With partner organizations involved in direct humanitarian support
  • With government entities (only when required by law or for coordination during disasters)
  • With technology providers (e.g., cloud storage, security, analytics), bound by strict privacy terms

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers hosted in the United States and Europe. We apply industry-standard safeguards and access controls to prevent unauthorized access or misuse.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

You can manage your preferences via your profile page or contact us directly.

6. Children’s Privacy

NOVA7 is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. International Use

By using NOVA7, you acknowledge your data may be processed in countries where WDC is active (U.S., Canada, Austria) and where our infrastructure partners operate.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review the policy regularly.

9. Contact Us

For questions, complaints, or to exercise your data rights, contact:
